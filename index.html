Fresh And Organic Product

Fresh and organic products are food items produced without synthetic chemicals such as pesticides and fertilizers, and are not processed using industrial solvents, irradiation, or genetic engineering. These products are typically grown using natural methods, aiming to preserve the environment and promote sustainable farming practices. Freshness indicates that the products are recently harvested and have minimal processing, ensuring they retain their natural flavors and nutritional value. Organic products are often preferred by consumers for their perceived health benefits and environmental friendliness.
